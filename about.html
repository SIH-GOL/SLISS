About SLISS

SLISS (Student Link of Integrated Smart System) is a comprehensive student management system designed to simplify administration and improve accessibility for schools and colleges. From admissions to fee management, academic records, and communication tools, SLISS connects students, teachers, and parents in one centralized platform, making education smarter and more efficient.
